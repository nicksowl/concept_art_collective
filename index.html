CONCEPT art collective has the purpose to promote experimental forms of contemporary art and support emerging artists by creating cultural events and growing networks of opportunities, relationships, and communication among artists, art enthusiasts, cultural professionals.

CONCEPT was created to enhance a variety of forms in experimental art in order to raise public awareness and to introduce local/contemporary art to people. We long for durational, crosspollinating relations with the participants in order to create an ecosystem of individuals concerning arts, form alliances across different collectives, bodies, and fields and so present many different art forms and their intersection.

Our primary aims include the fostering and promotion of local contemporary art by facilitating multidisciplinary events, exhibitions, performances, and workshops. Our main interest is to enhance different art forms in their intersection.Our thinking is rhizomatic, our curatorial apparatus is nomadic and tentacular.

CONCEPT is addressed to different targets, offering opportuni- ties and possibilities for new collaborations in order to spread new forms of experimental art approaches and develop new projects.
